The Beach Pier

The Pompano Beach Pier is perfect for early morning walks and watching the sunrise over the ocean. It's a popular spot for fishing, taking photos, and enjoying panoramic views of the shoreline. At night, the pier is beautifully lit, making it a must-see attraction for visitors.

Hillsboro Inlet Lighthouse

This historic lighthouse has guided ships safely for over a century. The black-and-white tower stands proudly at the inlet, with sweeping views of the Atlantic. Tours are available on select days, offering a fascinating glimpse into maritime history and stunning photo opportunities.

Pompano Beach Park

Pompano Beach Park is a great place to relax with family and friends. The park features soft sandy beaches, palm trees for shade, volleyball courts, picnic areas, and plenty of space to spread out. It's ideal for sunbathing, playing in the surf, or simply enjoying a beautiful Florida day.
